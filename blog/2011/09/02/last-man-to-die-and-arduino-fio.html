Last Man to Die and Arduino Fio

02 Sep '11

Last week the Last Mans and I put on a little run of our show as part of National Science Week.

Most exciting for me was the chance to refine the wearable interface I put together for Hanna.

I upgraded from a regular arduino, xbee shield and 6 AA batteries to an Arduino Fio and a LiPo battery. The Arduino Fio is super cool to use, it has a built-in xBee socket, and a charging circuit for the lithium polymer battery.
