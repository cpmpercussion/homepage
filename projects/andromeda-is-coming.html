Andromeda is Coming (Charles Martin and Alexander Hunter) are a Canberra-based duo utilising viola da gamba, percussion and live electronics.

Say hi on Facebook.

Notable Performances

  • Album launch (Ralph Wilson Theatre, Canberra), July 2017
  • Canberra Obscura (Ainslie and Gorman Arts Centre), May 2016.
  • Apocalyptic and Integrated: Art Not Apart, March 2016, Canberra, Australia
  • Magic Lantern Horror Show, National Portrait Gallery, Canberra, Australia
  • Electrofringe 2015, Sydney, Australia. (video)
  • Ainslie Arts Centre, 'Avoidance Behaviour', July 2015, Canberra, Australia. (video)
  • You Are Here 2015, Canberra, Australia
